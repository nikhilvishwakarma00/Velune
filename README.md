\## What is Velune?



Velune is a full-featured Android music app that connects to YouTube Music ‚ÄĒ no ads, no subscriptions, no compromises. It fetches real data from the YouTube Music InnerTube API, caches it locally, and plays it back with professional-grade audio features.



Built on a modern Kotlin stack with Jetpack Compose and Media3, Velune is designed for people who want the full YouTube Music experience without the YouTube Music app.



\---



\## Features



\### Core

\- \*\*Ad-Free Playback\*\* ‚ÄĒ zero interruptions, ever

\- \*\*Full Library Sync\*\* ‚ÄĒ Liked Songs, Playlists, Subscriptions, History

\- \*\*Offline Caching\*\* ‚ÄĒ smart local storage with encrypted Room database

\- \*\*Background Playback\*\* ‚ÄĒ battery-optimized MediaSession service



\### Audio

\- \*\*Gapless Playback\*\* ‚ÄĒ seamless track transitions

\- \*\*Crossfade\*\* ‚ÄĒ smooth blending between songs

\- \*\*Silence Skipping\*\* ‚ÄĒ auto-skip quiet gaps

\- \*\*Loudness Normalization\*\* ‚ÄĒ EBU R128 standard

\- \*\*Tempo \& Pitch Control\*\* ‚ÄĒ real-time manipulation

\- \*\*System EQ Bridge\*\* ‚ÄĒ integrates with any equalizer app



\### Visual \& Discovery

\- \*\*Material You (MD3)\*\* ‚ÄĒ UI adapts to album art color palette

\- \*\*Synced Lyrics\*\* ‚ÄĒ word-by-word playback with translation support

\- \*\*Discord Rich Presence\*\* ‚ÄĒ show what you're listening to

\- \*\*Year in Review\*\* ‚ÄĒ your personal music stats

\- \*\*Home Feed\*\* ‚ÄĒ personalised Quick Picks, Keep Listening, mood chips

\- \*\*Custom V Loader\*\* ‚ÄĒ animated Velune branding on every loading state
